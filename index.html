About Me

Hi, I’m Sunny, a wedding and lifestyle photographer based in Connecticut and serving Hartford and the surrounding areas. Photography has been my passion for as long as I can remember, and nothing brings me more joy than capturing the moments that mean the most to you.

I specialize in weddings, but I also love documenting other important milestones in your life, including engagements, family celebrations, and meaningful everyday moments. My goal is to create images that feel natural, emotional, and true to your story.

With an eye for detail and a love for authentic connections, I focus on capturing the perfect shot in every setting, from quiet intimate moments to joyful celebrations filled with energy. Every photograph is created with care, creativity, and heart.

I would be honored to help you preserve the memories you want to remember forever.
